A web domain is your websites address on the internet (e.g., www.yoursite.com). Hosting is the service that allows your website to be accessible on the internet. Hosting provides the space and resources for your site to live online.

It depends on your websites technology stack. Linux servers are ideal for PHP-based websites (e.g., WordPress, Drupal) while Windows servers work better for ASP.NET and Microsoft SQL-based websites.

Both platforms are excellent for cloud hosting. However, Google Cloud may be a bit faster in terms of performance for certain workloads, while AWS offers a wider range of services and tools. It ultimately depends on your specific needs.

Organic SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps improve your websites visibility in search engines, driving more organic traffic to your site. It builds credibility and trust with users, improving your rankings and ensuring long-term success without relying on paid ads.
